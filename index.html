About La Mesa Eco Park

La Mesa Eco Park is a relaxing outdoor destination in Quezon City, a hot spot great for hiking, picnics, photography, workouts, nature bonding, and family activities.

  • Opening Hours: 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM
  • Entrance Fee: ₱50 - ₱100
  • Location: La Mesa, Quezon City
